About This Project

Decoding Nature, the base course of this project, analyzes what has happend in the past, or what is happening in present, and understand it, allowing us to better understand future events. As we carried on with the course, and started understanding how to 'decode' and 'understand' nature, one question occurred. "Would I be able to 'decode the nature' of NYUAD?" With this, I started this project with the plan to get inputs of students' thoughts by displaying certain keywords and displaying what sutdents think.
